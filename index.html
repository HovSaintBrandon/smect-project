About Smect Consultants

The Organization

Smect Consultants Company Limited is a professional business development firm with proven expertise in Programme Design and Development; Project Planning and Management, Organizational Capacity Assessment and training; and Gender Audit.

Our portfolio of services encompasses social development services such as Social Research, Monitoring and Evaluation, Public Information, Advocacy and Awareness Campaigns. We provide expert and professional consultancy services on the African continent generally and specifically in Eastern and Southern Africa region.

Our Vision

To be a world class consulting firm recognized both locally and internationally as a professional service provider that accomplishes quality and timely service through a multi-disciplinary approach.

Our Mission

To offer practical, effective and competitive solutions to business entities, Governments and Non-Governmental Organizations to enable them achieve maximum efficiencies, productivity, business growth and profitability and stakeholder satisfaction.

Our Approach

We offer ourselves upon understanding the client's needs and aspirations, service excellence and deliver solutions which are practical, effective and competitive. We focus on assisting clients in achieving maximum efficiencies and productivity, business growth and profitability and stakeholder satisfaction.
